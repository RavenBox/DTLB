Classics

consists of applying a single lash extension to a natural lash. Only apply as many extensions as healthy natural eyelashes you have.

Hybrids

A mixture of classic extensions and volume, 50% of classic extensions and another 50% of volume are usually applied, it is possible to obtain a more voluminous effect than with classic eyelashes, but without reaching such a striking volume.

Volume

It consists of safely applying 2 (2D) to more (3D, 4D) super fine eyelashes for each natural eyelash, these allow us to create a much more abundant effect.
Special for those people who do not have eyelashes very populated natural or for those who want a voluminous effect.

Microshading

It is a semi-permanent shading of the eyebrows, which fills spaces with suitable pigments at the epidermal level. This technique is ideal for lightly populated eyebrows without definition or with some scar. It is suitable for all people with healthy skin.

Shading

It is a way to give shape and a make-up effect to your eyebrow.
It is used very frequently as a complement to microblading because both techniques combined are very effective. Combining these two techniques we achieve incredible results since we obtain the benefits of both techniques in a single treatment.

Microblading

is a good option if you want to try to modify your eyebrows, both in size, thickness or shape, in a natural way. It is done hair by hair and is temporary, since being tattooed on the epidermis lasts between one and two years.
